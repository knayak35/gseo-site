Research
 
&
 
Publication
 
Policy
 
Purpose
 
GSEO   is  committed  to  promoting  academic  excellence  by  providing  a  platform  for  students  to  share  
research,
 
educational
 
content,
 
and
 
scholarly
 
perspectives
 
with
 
a
 
global
 
audience.
 
This  policy  outlines  the  standards  and  procedures  governing  all  content  published  through  GSEO.  
Eligible
 
Content
 
GSEO  accepts  submissions  in  a  wide  range  of  academic  disciplines,  including  but  not  limited  to:  
●  Physics  ●  Chemistry  ●  Biology  ●  Mathematics  ●  Computer  Science  &  Artificial  Intelligence  ●  Engineering  ●  Finance  ●  Economics  ●  International  Relations  ●  Psychology  &  Mental  Health  ●  Environmental  Science  ●  Humanities  and  Social  Sciences  
Submission
 
Requirements
 
All  submissions  must:  
●  Be  written  in  clear  and  professional  English.  ●  Demonstrate  originality  and  intellectual  contribution.  ●  Include  appropriate  citations  and  references  where  applicable.  ●  Adhere  to  principles  of  academic  honesty  and  integrity.  ●  Avoid  plagiarism,  fabrication,  or  misrepresentation  of  information.  
Authors  retain  responsibility  for  the  accuracy  of  their  work.

Review
 
Process
 
All  submissions  undergo  a  review  process  conducted  by  members  of  the  Academic  Division.  
The  review  process  may  evaluate:  
●  Accuracy  of  information  ●  Clarity  of  communication  ●  Quality  of  research  ●  Citation  practices  ●  Relevance  to  GSEOʼs  mission  
GSEO  reserves  the  right  to  request  revisions  prior  to  publication.  
Academic  Integrity  
GSEO  maintains  a  zero-tolerance  policy  toward:  
●  Plagiarism  ●  Data  fabrication  ●  Citation  manipulation  ●  Misrepresentation  of  authorship  
Any  submission  found  to  violate  academic  integrity  standards  may  be  rejected  or  removed  from  publication.  
AI
 
Usage
 
Policy
 
The  use  of  artificial  intelligence  tools  is  permitted  as  a  supportive  aid  for  brainstorming,  editing,  and  
improving
 
clarity.
 
However:  
●  Authors  must  remain  the  primary  creators  of  their  work.  ●  AI-generated  content  must  be  reviewed  for  accuracy.  ●  Authors  are  fully  responsible  for  all  published  material.  
Submissions  consisting  primarily  of  unedited  AI-generated  content  may  be  rejected.  
Copyright
 
Authors  retain  ownership  of  their  original  work.  
By  submitting  content  to  GSEO,  authors  grant  GSEO  permission  to  publish,  distribute,  and  promote  the  
content
 
through
 
its
 
platforms
 
while
 
crediting
 
the
 
original
 
author.
